Guide / Notifications

Dismiss once, gone everywhere.

Phone notifications appear on the desktop the moment they land, and the reverse holds for dismissal: dismissing a notification on either device clears it on both -- no stale mirrored notification lingering on the desktop after you've already cleared it on the phone.

Settings → Notification mirroring → Grant access

Android asks you to enable Connectible under its system Notification Access settings -- a one-time setup, on the phone only.

Notifications mirror automatically

They show up under the desktop's Notifications panel with no further configuration.

Manage anytime

The same settings row's Manage button jumps back to Android's notification access settings if you want to revoke it.

This is mirroring, not remote control of notifications beyond dismissal -- no replying or triggering a notification action from the desktop.
